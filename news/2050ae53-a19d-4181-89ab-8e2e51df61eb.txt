# Past Exams and Solutions

The SOA provides copies of prior exams and model solutions as an aid for candidate study. Candidates should be aware that exam syllabi are constantly evolving and therefore not every prior question is relevant for the current exam. Even so, the questions and solutions do provide guidance regarding the type of questions asked and appropriate responses.

Past exams up to five years are available to candidates upon request. Exams P, FM, FAM, SRM and GIINT will not be released. Please contact [Customer Service](https://help.soa.org/) for assistance.
